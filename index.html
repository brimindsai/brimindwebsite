BriMinds AI Olympiad — India

From your classroom.
To the world.

Annual finals. Continuous qualifying. India's first AI Olympiad — giving every CBSE student a path from their school to the Global BriMinds AI Championship.

Round 1
School Level
Every registered CBSE school runs internal qualifying rounds throughout the year. Open to all students Grade 3–12.
Round 2
City Level
Top qualifiers compete citywide. Mumbai. Delhi. Bangalore. Hyderabad. Chennai. City champions advance to State.
Round 3
State Level
City champions battle for State honours. One State Champion per grade band. 28 states, one winner each.
Round 4
India National Final
State champions meet at the India National AI Championship. The brightest young AI minds in the country. National Champions advance to the Global stage.
Round 5
South Asian Regional
India's National Champions compete against national champions from UAE, Singapore, and Hong Kong. The regional proving ground.
The Final
BriMinds Global AI Championship
One annual event. Seven countries. The world's best young AI minds. India's champion on the world stage. This is where legends are made.
